Process for exercising data protection rights

In order to exercise your data protection rights, you may contact Mercantus as described in the Contact Us section below. We take each request seriously. We will comply with your request to the extent required by applicable law. We will not be able to respond to a request if we no longer hold your Personal Data. If you feel that you have not received a satisfactory response from us, you may consult with the data protection authority in your country. For your protection, we may need to verify your identity before responding to your request, such as verifying that the email address from which you send the request matches your email address that we have on file. If you are a Customer of a Mercantus User, please direct your requests directly to the User. For example, if you are making, or have made, a purchase from a merchant using Mercantus as a payment processor, and you have a request that is related to the payment information that you provided as part of the purchase transaction, then you should address your request directly to the merchant.
